‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: My notes disappeared. How can I recover them?

A: Obsidian Notes stores all data locally on your device. If notes disappeared:

  • Check if you accidentally deleted them
  • Restart the app completely
  • Ensure you haven't switched themes (notes are theme-independent)
  • If the issue persists, contact support immediately
Q: How do I change themes?

A: Open the app ‚Üí Tap Settings ‚Üí Select Theme ‚Üí Choose from Purple, Blue, Green, or Orange

Q: Is my data secure?

A: Yes! All your notes are stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers, and no analytics or tracking is performed.

Q: How do I backup my notes?

A: Since data is stored locally, ensure your device is backed up to iCloud or iTunes. Your notes will be restored when you restore your device backup.

Q: The app is running slowly. What should I do?

A: Try these steps:

  • Close the app completely and reopen it
  • Restart your device
  • Ensure you have sufficient storage space
  • Update to the latest iOS version
