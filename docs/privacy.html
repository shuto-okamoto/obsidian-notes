3. How We Use Your Data

The only data processed by Obsidian Notes is:

  • Your Notes: Stored locally to display and edit your content
  • App Settings: Stored locally to remember your theme and preferences

All processing happens exclusively on your device.

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT share any data with third parties.

Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it yourself.

5. Data Storage and Security

Storage Location

  • All data is stored in your device's secure sandboxed storage
  • Data is protected by iOS built-in encryption
  • No cloud storage or external servers are used

Security Measures

  • App sandbox protection (iOS standard)
  • Device-level encryption
  • No network communication for data storage
  • Regular security updates through App Store

6. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

Since we don't collect personal data, traditional GDPR rights don't apply. However:

  • Right to Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • Right to Portability: You can export your notes anytime
  • Right to Deletion: Uninstall the app to delete all data
  • Right to Rectification: Edit your notes directly in the app

7. Data Retention

  • Data is retained until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • No automatic data deletion
  • No data backup to external services
  • Your device's backup settings control data backup

8. International Data Transfers

Not Applicable: Since no data leaves your device, there are no international transfers.

9. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Obsidian Notes is safe for children:

  • No online interactions or chat features
  • No advertisements or third-party content
  • App Store rating: 4+ (suitable for all ages)

10. Cookies and Tracking

We use NO cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics tools.
